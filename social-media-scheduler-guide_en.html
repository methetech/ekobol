Using Ekobol's Social Media Scheduler

By The Ekobol Marketing Team on July 23, 2025

Social media is indispensable as a marketing tool for e-commerce businesses, but consistently posting across multiple platforms can be time-consuming. Ekobol's Social Media Scheduler allows you to easily create and plan your social media posts from one centralized location, even generating content with AI assistance. Here's how to use this tool:

1. Accessing the Social Media Scheduler

  • Log in to your Ekobol dashboard.
  • Navigate to the "Marketing Tools" section in the left sidebar navigation.
  • You'll find the "Social Media Scheduler" section towards the bottom of the page.

2. Creating Your Post Content

To compose your social media post:

  • Type your desired message into the "Post Content" text area.
  • (Optional) Generate Content with AI: Provide some keywords, such as a product name or theme, to help the AI draft your post or refine existing text. Click the "Generate with AI" button below the text area.
  • Look for an option to upload relevant product images or other media (not yet functional in static HTML, but would be present in an app).

3. Scheduling Your Post

Once your post content is ready, set the date and time for it to be published:

  • Click on the "Schedule Date & Time" field.
  • Select your desired time from the date and time picker that appears. The AI can even suggest optimal times when your audience is most active (advanced feature).

4. Selecting Platforms to Publish To

Choose which social media platforms you want your post to go live on:

  • Check the checkboxes for supported platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter.
  • Ekobol will automatically publish your post across all selected platforms.

5. Scheduling the Post

After configuring all the details:

  • Click the "Schedule Post" button.
  • Your post will now be queued to be published automatically at the designated date and time.

Monitoring Performance:

After your post goes live, you can track its performance using Ekobol's analytics section. Monitor metrics such as reach, engagement, and traffic driven to your website or product listings. This data will help you refine your future social media strategies.

Ekobol's Social Media Scheduler simplifies the way you manage your social media presence, allowing you to market more effectively and reclaim your most valuable asset: time.
