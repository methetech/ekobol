Understanding Your Ekobol Dashboard

By The Ekobol Support Team on July 18, 2025

The Ekobol dashboard is the central nervous system of your e-commerce empire. It's designed to give you a single place to monitor sales, manage inventory, and receive AI recommendations, among many other functions. This guide will walk you through the main sections of your dashboard.

1. Overview Cards

At the very top of your dashboard, you'll find summary cards that provide a quick snapshot of your business:

  • Total Sales: Shows your gross revenue over a specific period (typically the last 30 days).
  • Total Orders: The total number of orders processed in the same period.
  • Total Profit: Your estimated net profit for the same period.

These cards often include trend indicators compared to previous periods, providing a quick performance assessment.

2. Channel Performance Section

This section breaks down the performance of each of your connected marketplaces. For each channel (Etsy, Trendyol, Hepsiburada, Website, etc.), you'll see:

  • Channel-Specific Sales: The total sales from that channel and its percentage contribution to your overall sales.
  • Sales Charts: Mini-charts showing sales trends over time.
  • Recent Orders: A quick list of the latest orders from that channel. You can jump to order details from here for more specifics.

3. Quick Stats

This card provides bullet-point summaries of various aspects of your business:

  • Top Performing Product: Quickly identifies which of your products is performing best.
  • Best Channel (by revenue): Shows your top-earning channel.
  • Overall Average Order Value: Helps you understand how much you're earning per order on average.
  • Low Stock Alerts: Highlights products that might need reordering soon.

4. AI Recommendations for Growth

This is one of Ekobol's most powerful features. Our AI engine constantly analyzes your sales data and provides you with actionable insights and growth strategies. Recommendations might include:

  • Suggestions for launching new ad campaigns.
  • Advice on optimizing product descriptions or pricing.
  • Identified growth opportunities for specific products or channels.

You can quickly jump to the relevant marketing or product management tools to implement these recommendations.

5. Navigation and Other Areas

The top and left navigation bars of your dashboard lead you to other crucial areas of the platform:

  • Orders: To manage all your customer orders.
  • Products: For adding, editing, and synchronizing your product catalog.
  • Marketing Tools: For AI-powered marketing campaigns and SEO optimization.
  • Analytics: For more in-depth trend analysis and live data.
  • Finances: To track your earnings, expenses, and subscription details.

The Ekobol dashboard is designed to simplify your business, help you make better decisions, and ultimately scale your e-commerce operations. Make sure to regularly review your data and don't hesitate to explore how AI can work for your business!
