Optimizing Your Pricing with AI in Ekobol

By The Ekobol Product Team on July 19, 2025

In the competitive world of e-commerce, setting the right pricing strategy can significantly impact your sales and profitability. Ekobol's AI-powered Pricing Optimization tool provides you with intelligent price recommendations by analyzing market dynamics, competition, and demand trends. Here's how to best utilize this tool:

1. Accessing Pricing Optimization

  • Log in to your Ekobol dashboard.
  • Navigate to the Products section and select the product whose price you wish to optimize.
  • On the product editing page, you will find an "Optimize with AI" button next to the "Price" field.

2. Receiving the AI Recommendation

When you click the "Optimize with AI" button, our artificial intelligence begins to process available data:

  • Competitor Analysis: It compares current prices of similar products across your selected marketplaces.
  • Demand Forecasting: It assesses demand based on your historical sales data and market trends.
  • Profit Margin Calculation: It takes into account the product's cost and marketplace commissions.

Within a few seconds, Ekobol will present you with a new recommended price. This price aims to balance staying competitive while maximizing your profit.

3. Applying the Recommended Price

If you wish to accept the AI-suggested price:

  • You will see the price field automatically update.
  • Click the "Save Product" button on the product editing page to save the change. Ekobol will then synchronize this updated price across all your connected channels.

4. Understanding Your Pricing Strategies

While AI recommendations provide a solid starting point, it's essential that they align with your overall business goals.

  • Maximum Profit: AI will often suggest prices that target the highest possible profit margin.
  • Maximum Sales Volume: If you prioritize higher sales volume, you might consider setting a price slightly lower than what the AI suggests.
  • Market Positioning: Are you positioning your product as a premium item or a budget-friendly option? Adjust AI recommendations based on your brand positioning.

5. Monitoring and Refining Performance

Regularly monitoring the impact of price changes is crucial:

  • Dashboard & Analytics: Use the data in your Ekobol dashboard and analytics section to see how AI pricing influences your sales volume, conversion rates, and overall profitability.
  • A/B Testing: Consider A/B testing different price points or AI recommendations to find what works best for you.

By leveraging Ekobol's AI Pricing Optimization tool, you can maintain or increase your profitability while staying competitive. It's a data-driven advantage that helps you scale your e-commerce business smarter.
