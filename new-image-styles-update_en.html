Feature Update: New Image Generation Styles

By The Ekobol Product Team on July 17, 2025

At Ekobol, we understand the importance of e-commerce sellers showcasing their products in the best possible light. That's why we're thrilled to introduce three exciting new styles for our AI Image Generator. These updates will allow you to customize and differentiate your product visuals like never before.

New Image Styles

Here are the new styles added to our AI Image Generator:

1. Minimalist Studio

For those who crave a clean, sleek, and distraction-free aesthetic, the "Minimalist Studio" style generates a simple backdrop that lets your product stand out. It's ideal when you want to focus on the details and quality of your item.

2. Natural Ambiance

If you wish to display your products in a more organic and inviting setting, "Natural Ambiance" is perfect. This style creates relaxing scenes filled with natural light and textures, giving a sense of how your product might be used in the real world.

3. Futuristic Geometric

For products that are more modern, innovative, or tech-oriented, the "Futuristic Geometric" style offers a striking, contemporary backdrop with dynamic lines and abstract shapes. It's excellent for highlighting the cutting-edge aspect of your product.

How to Use?

Using these new styles is incredibly easy! Simply navigate to the AI Image Generator in your Ekobol dashboard, upload your product image, and select from the expanded list of style options. Our AI will generate stunning visuals in your chosen style within seconds.

We believe these new styles will further help your products stand out in the marketplace and reinforce your brand's unique identity. Thank you for choosing Ekobol!
