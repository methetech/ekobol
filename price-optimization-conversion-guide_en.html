Ekobol's AI-powered analytics and pricing optimization tools can help you fine-tune your prices to boost conversion. Here's how to get started:

1. Understanding Conversion Rates

Conversion rate is the number of sales divided by the total number of visitors who viewed your product listing. A high conversion rate means your product and its price are resonating well with your target audience.

  • Data in Ekobol Dashboard: Monitor "Conversion Rate" metrics in your dashboard or "Analytics" section. This will help you understand your baseline performance before and after any major pricing changes.

2. Utilizing AI Pricing Optimization

Ekobol's AI Pricing Optimization tool is designed to optimize not just profitability but also sales volume. When using this tool to improve conversion rate, consider:

  • Competitive Positioning: The AI dynamically suggests prices relative to competitors on your marketplace to ensure you remain competitive. A competitive price is fundamental for attracting potential buyers.
  • Flexibility and Responsiveness: The AI can adjust prices in response to changes in demand, stock levels, and competitor pricing. This flexibility allows you to always offer the best price for optimal conversion.

3. Implementing Pricing Experiments (A/B Testing)

Leverage pricing A/B tests to determine which price point leads to the highest conversion rate.

  • Different Pricing Tiers: Select a few variations of the AI-suggested price (e.g., AI suggested price, AI suggested price +5%, AI suggested price -5%).
  • Apply on Channel: Apply these different pricings to different listings of the same product on one or more marketplaces for a specific period.
  • Analyze Results: Once the test period concludes, use Ekobol analytics to determine which price point generated the highest conversion rate, revenue, and profit.

Ekobol can assist you in setting up and analyzing such tests, using AI to pinpoint your ideal price point.

4. Evaluating Campaigns and Promotions

Pricing optimization isn't always about the base price of the product. Use AI analytics to understand how campaigns and promotions impact conversion rates:

  • Conversion Impact: Use AI analytics to see how past discounts or campaign prices led to a boost in conversion rates.
  • Optimal Discount Rates: The AI might even suggest the best discount rate or promotional strategy for a specific product.

By continuously reviewing your pricing strategy and leveraging the power of Ekobol's AI, you will not only maximize your profit but also optimize your ability to convert visitors into paying customers.
