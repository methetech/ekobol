How to Use Ekobol's SEO Optimization Tools

By The Ekobol Marketing Team on July 22, 2025

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is vital for your products to be discovered by potential customers, both on marketplaces and general search engines. Ekobol's AI-powered SEO Optimization Tools simplify this process by analyzing and optimizing your listings for keywords, titles, and descriptions. Here’s how to use these tools:

1. Accessing SEO Optimization Tools

  • Log in to your Ekobol dashboard.
  • Navigate to the "Marketing Tools" section in the left sidebar navigation.
  • You'll find the "SEO Optimization Tools" section on the page.

2. Selecting a Product and Initiating Analysis

Choose the product you wish to perform SEO analysis for:

  • Select your product from the "Select Product for SEO Analysis" dropdown.
  • Click the "Analyze SEO & Suggest Keywords" button.
  • The AI will begin analyzing your product's current description, title, and category. It will also examine keyword trends and competition for similar products across marketplaces and general search engines.

3. Reviewing AI-Suggested Keywords

Once the analysis is complete, Ekobol will present you with a list of suggested keywords:

  • Each keyword is usually presented with its estimated search volume (high, medium, low) and competition level (high, medium, low).
  • Focus on the most relevant and high-potential keywords on the list.

4. Utilizing Meta Description Suggestions

Along with keyword suggestions, the AI often provides a draft of an optimized meta description—a brief summary that appears under your product in search engine results.

  • This meta description is designed to incorporate your keywords and product benefits to entice potential customers to click on your listing.
  • Edit it to match your own brand voice and ensure it is clear and persuasive.

5. Optimizing Your Listing

To optimize your product listing using the suggested keywords and meta description:

  • Product Title: Incorporate some of your most relevant and high-volume keywords into your title. Make sure it still sounds natural and appealing.
  • Product Description: Naturally sprinkle the suggested keywords throughout your product description and bullet points. Be benefit-driven and highlight the most important aspects of your product.
  • Tags/Labels: Utilize the suggested keywords for your marketplace tags.
  • Meta Description: Apply the AI-generated meta description directly or adjust it as needed.

Remember to save your product listing after making these changes.

Monitoring Performance:

Regularly track the performance of your optimized listings using the "Analytics" section in your Ekobol dashboard. Look for improvements in keyword rankings, increases in product views, and boosts in conversion rates. As the AI continues to learn, it will keep providing further SEO recommendations to enhance your performance.

Ekobol's SEO Optimization Tools empower you with powerful AI-driven insights to improve your visibility in the digital marketplace, helping more potential customers find your products.
