Setting Up Your Ekobol Profile

By The Ekobol Support Team on July 18, 2025

Setting up your Ekobol profile correctly is crucial for a smooth experience and personalized insights. This guide will help you configure your account and manage your personal information.

1. Accessing Profile Settings

  • Log in to your Ekobol dashboard.
  • Click on your profile picture or username, typically located in the top right corner.
  • From the dropdown menu, select "Profile Settings" or "My Account."

2. Updating Your Basic Information

On the profile settings page, you can edit your essential details such as your name, email address, and contact information.

  • First and Last Name: Enter your first and last name.
  • Email Address: This is your primary email for account notifications. Update it if necessary.
  • Phone Number: Optionally add a phone number for communication purposes.
  • Remember to click the "Save" or "Update" button after making any changes.

3. Changing Your Password

It's good practice to change your password regularly to ensure the security of your account.

  • Locate the "Password" or "Security" section.
  • Enter your current password.
  • Enter and confirm your new password. Remember to use a strong password (mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols).
  • Click the "Change Password" button to confirm the changes.

4. Marketplace Preferences and Business Information

Ekobol offers tailored experiences based on your business's needs.

  • Marketplace Role: Indicate which marketplaces you are active on (Trendyol, Etsy, Amazon, etc.). This helps us provide more relevant tips and features.
  • Business Name: Enter the name of your business, if applicable.
  • Default Currency/Language: Set your preferred default currency and language for your dashboard.

Updating this information allows Ekobol to better optimize your workflow and present you with the most relevant data.

5. Notification Settings

Control the types of notifications you wish to receive from Ekobol.

  • You can enable or disable options like sales alerts, low stock notifications, AI recommendations, or general updates.
  • Save your preferences.

Regularly reviewing and updating your profile will ensure you get the most out of Ekobol. Remember, accurate and up-to-date information is fundamental for our AI-powered tools to deliver the most precise and beneficial insights for your business.
