Best Practices for AI Product Descriptions in Ekobol

By The Ekobol Product Team on July 19, 2025

Ekobol's AI Product Description Writer is a revolutionary tool that saves you time and effort by generating catchy and SEO-friendly product copy. However, to get the best results, it's important to understand how the AI responds to input. Here are the best practices for AI descriptions:

1. Provide Clear and Concise Product Information

The initial information you feed the AI will directly impact the quality of the description it generates.

  • Key Features: List what the product is, what it's made of (material), its dimensions, and any unique functionalities.
  • Benefits: State how the product solves a customer's problem or improves their life. Use phrases like "This T-shirt provides breathable comfort all day long" instead of "This T-shirt is soft."
  • Target Audience: Who is the product for? (e.g., "new mothers," "outdoor adventurers," "minimalist home decor enthusiasts").
  • Keywords: Include 3-5 keywords you envision for your product. The AI will try to naturally incorporate them.

2. Specify Your Desired Tone

The AI can adapt to the language and instructions you provide. Use hints to specify the tone of your description:

  • "Generate a formal and informative description."
  • "Use a fun and friendly tone."
  • "Create a luxurious feel."

3. Utilize the Marketplace Selection

Ekobol's AI can optimize for the algorithms and audiences of different marketplaces like Trendyol, Etsy, and Amazon.

  • Make sure to select your target marketplace when generating the description. This helps the AI consider relevant SEO factors and style guidelines.

4. Refine the Initial Draft

Once the AI generates a draft, take the time to perfect it:

  • Human Touch: Edit the copy to align with your unique brand voice and tone. The AI provides a great starting point, but the final polish should always come from you.
  • Accuracy Check: Ensure all features and benefits mentioned are accurate and up-to-date for your product.
  • Readability: Read the description aloud. Does it flow well? Is it easy to skim? Use bullet points and short paragraphs.
  • Call to Action: End with a clear call to action (e.g., "Shop now," "Add to cart") telling the customer what you want them to do next.

5. Monitor Performance with A/B Testing

Leverage A/B testing to monitor the performance of different AI-generated descriptions or your edited versions. Use your analytics data to determine which titles lead to more clicks or which descriptions result in higher conversion rates.

By using Ekobol's AI Description Writer with these best practices, you will not only save time but also create more effective and appealing listings for your products, ultimately boosting your sales.
