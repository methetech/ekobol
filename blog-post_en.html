Introducing the Partner Plan: We Build, You Create

By The Ekobol Team on July 15, 2025

At Ekobol, we believe that to truly reach the potential of e-commerce, business founders should focus on creating products, not managing the complexities of digital operations. This philosophy has led us to introduce the Partner Plan, our most comprehensive offering designed to empower both established brands and budding entrepreneurs.

What is the Partner Plan?

The Partner Plan is a unique partnership based on a simple profit-sharing model: 50/50. While you produce and ship your amazing products, we manage every aspect of your digital presence and marketing efforts. This means you can scale your business without getting bogged down in juggling platforms or learning the intricacies of AI tools.

What's Included?

With the Partner Plan, Ekobol handles every detail of your digital business, including:

  • Professional Brand & Website Build: We create your brand identity and dedicated e-commerce website from the ground up.
  • Full Digital Marketing Management: We run all your marketing strategies, including SEO, paid ad campaigns, social media management, and content creation.
  • Comprehensive Operations Management: We handle day-to-day operations, including product listings, multi-marketplace synchronization, inventory automation, customer service, and marketplace support.
  • AI-Powered Optimization: All our advanced AI tools, such as price optimization and AI-generated descriptions, are fully leveraged by us to generate value for you.
  • Dedicated Growth Partner: You'll have an assigned team working closely with you to continuously ensure your success and help you hit your business goals.

Why Choose the Partner Plan?

This plan is perfect for busy founders whose time or expertise is better spent on product development rather than tackling all aspects of digital marketing or platform management. With the Partner Plan, you get:

  • Maximum Growth, Minimum Effort: Leverage our expertise to expand your business, allowing you to focus on what you do best: innovation.
  • Fully Managed Solution: Say goodbye to digital fatigue. Everything is taken care of.
  • Shared Success: Our revenue-sharing model means your success is our success. We are motivated to drive your growth.
