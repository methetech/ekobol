Viewing Your Ekobol Invoices

By The Ekobol Finance Team on July 21, 2025

Ekobol's billing system is designed to be transparent and easily accessible. You can view all your past invoices and payment statements directly from your dashboard. Here's how:

1. Accessing the Finance Section

  • Log in to your Ekobol dashboard.
  • Click on the "Finances" section in the left sidebar navigation.

2. Locating Your Billing History

Once on the Finances page, you'll notice it's divided into several sections. To access your invoice history:

  • Look for headings such as "Subscription & Billing" or "Recent Transactions."
  • You will typically find a link like "View Full Statement â†’" or similar, which allows you to view your complete billing statement. Click on this link.

3. Viewing and Filtering Invoices

On the full statement page, you will find a detailed list of all your past transactions and invoices.

  • Date Range Filter: Use the date range filters at the top of the page to view invoices for a specific month, quarter, or year.
  • Transaction Type: You can apply filters to see only income, expenses, or specific types of transactions.
  • Individual Invoices: By clicking on each invoice entry, you can view detailed invoice information, such as order specifics, service breakdowns, and associated fees.

4. Downloading Invoices

You can easily download any invoice or statement in PDF format for your records.

  • On the main statement page, use the "Download Report" button to download the entire statement.
  • Within an individual invoice view, you will find a separate download button to download that specific invoice.

These downloaded invoices can be used for your accounting records or tax purposes.
