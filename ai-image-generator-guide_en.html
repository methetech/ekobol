How to Use the AI Image Generator

By The Ekobol Support Team on July 18, 2025

Ekobol's AI Image Generator is a powerful tool designed to transform your product photos into professional, marketplace-ready visuals. This guide will walk you through how to use this feature most effectively.

1. Accessing the AI Image Generator

From your dashboard, navigate to the Products section and select a product you wish to edit, or add a new product. On the product details page, you'll find the AI Image Generator option under "Product Images."

2. Uploading Your Product Image

Once you access the generator, the first step is to upload a photo of your product.

  • Click the "Upload Image" button.
  • Choose the product photo you wish to use from your computer. A good quality starting image will yield the best AI results.
  • Wait for the image to upload.

3. Choosing an Image Style

After your image is uploaded, you will be presented with various style options that dictate how the AI will transform the background and overall aesthetic.

  • Browse through the available styles: You'll find options like Minimalist Studio, Natural Ambiance, Futuristic Geometric, and more.
  • Select the style that best suits your product and brand. Each style will give your visual a distinct feel.

4. Generating the AI Image

Once you've chosen your style:

  • Click the "Generate with AI" button.
  • Wait a few seconds while the AI generates the image.
  • The generated image will appear in the preview area, automatically transformed according to your selected style.

5. Reviewing and Saving Your Image

Carefully review the generated image. If you are satisfied:

  • Click the "Save" button. The image will be added to your product's media gallery.
  • If you're not satisfied, you can choose a different style or click "Regenerate" (if available) to try again.

Tips and Best Practices:

  • Use High-Resolution Images: Provide clear, well-lit original photos for better AI results.
  • Experiment with Different Styles: Try various options to see which style best showcases your product.
  • Utilize Background Removal: Many styles automatically remove the background, but you can also use this option separately if needed.

Ekobol's AI Image Generator empowers you to elevate your product visuals with ease and speed. Impress potential customers with stunning imagery and boost your sales!
