Creating AI-Powered Marketing Campaigns in Ekobol

By The Ekobol Marketing Team on July 22, 2025

Ekobol's AI Ad Campaign Generator helps you optimize your marketing efforts by creating high-performing ad copy and target audiences for your products. This guide will walk you through how to launch effective campaigns using artificial intelligence.

1. Accessing the AI Ad Campaign Generator

  • Log in to your Ekobol dashboard.
  • Navigate to the "Marketing Tools" section in the left sidebar navigation.
  • You'll find the "AI Ad Campaign Generator" section at the top of the page.

2. Inputting Campaign Information

You'll need to provide some basic information for the AI to generate your campaign:

  • Select Product for Campaign: Choose a specific product you wish to promote from the dropdown menu. The AI will analyze its features and benefits.
  • Campaign Goal: Select the primary objective of your campaign:
    • Increase Sales: Focuses on driving direct purchases.
    • Increase Visibility: Aims to reach more people and boost product impressions.
    • Build Brand Awareness: Grows overall recognition of your brand name and offerings.

3. Generating the Campaign Draft

Once you've entered the information:

  • Click the "Generate Campaign Draft" button.
  • The AI will generate a draft ad copy and suggested target audience parameters optimized for your product and goal, all within seconds.

4. Reviewing and Editing the Generated Copy and Audience

Carefully review the AI-generated ad copy and audience suggestions.

  • Ad Copy: Examine the suggested headlines, descriptions, and calls to action. You can edit them to match your brand's voice and specific campaign requirements.
  • Target Audience: The AI will suggest an ideal audience profile based on factors like demographics, interests, and behaviors. You can make further adjustments within your ad platform accordingly.

Tip: Always tailor the AI output to your specific ad platform's (e.g., Google Ads, Meta Ads) guidelines and best practices.

5. Launching Your Campaign

Once you are satisfied with the copy and audience:

  • Copy the generated ad text.
  • Paste it into your preferred advertising platform (e.g., Trendyol Ads, Google Ads, Facebook Ads).
  • Apply the AI-suggested target audience parameters and launch your campaign!

Monitoring Performance and Optimization:

After launching your campaign, it's crucial to monitor its performance from your Ekobol analytics dashboard. Track sales, click-through rates (CTR), conversion rates, and return on investment (ROI). You can use this data to refine your input for future AI campaigns.

Ekobol's AI-powered ad campaign generator allows you to create your marketing strategies faster and more effectively, enabling you to focus on attracting more customers and growing your business.
