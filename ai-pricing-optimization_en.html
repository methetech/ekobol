5 Ways AI Can Optimize Your Pricing on Trendyol

By The Ekobol Team on July 16, 2025

Trendyol, as one of Turkey's largest e-commerce platforms, offers an immense opportunity to reach millions of buyers. However, in this highly competitive marketplace, setting the right pricing strategy is crucial. While traditional methods of tracking prices can be challenging, Artificial Intelligence (AI) can provide you with a significant advantage. Let's explore 5 ways Ekobol's AI-powered pricing optimization tools can boost your sales on Trendyol.

1. Monitor Competitor Pricing in Real-Time

There might be hundreds, even thousands, of competitors selling your product on Trendyol. Manually tracking their prices is impossible. AI can monitor competitor pricing instantly and suggest the most competitive price point for you. This ensures you don't price too high or unnecessarily low.

2. Analyze Demand and Conversion Rates

AI not only tracks competitor prices but also analyzes the demand curves and conversion rates of your products. By evaluating your sales volume and profit margins at specific price points, it helps you dynamically determine the ideal price that will generate the most sales and profit.

3. Implement Dynamic Pricing Strategies

Thanks to AI, you can automatically adjust your prices based on the time of day, stock levels, competitor changes, or special campaign periods. For instance, you can slightly increase your prices when stock is low or a competitor is out of stock, or apply discounts during off-peak hours to boost demand.

4. Optimize Your Profit Margins

Offering the lowest price isn't always the best strategy. AI helps you maximize your profit margins by balancing both sales volume and the profit you gain from each sale. It considers your costs, marketplace commissions, and shipping fees to provide you with the most profitable pricing recommendations.

5. Conduct Pricing Experiments with A/B Testing

Manually A/B testing to find which price performs best can be time-consuming and complex. AI can simultaneously test different pricing strategies, quickly identify which price points lead to the highest conversion rates or profitability, and provide you with recommendations based on this data.

Ekobol's AI-powered pricing optimization tools are a powerful ally for your success on Trendyol. Instead of relying solely on intuition for pricing, you can now make data-driven smart decisions, ensuring higher sales and profitability.
